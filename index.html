About

India is a knowledge economy, one that creates, disseminates, and uses knowledge to enhance its growth and development. The founders of Vijaya Vittala Group of Institutions believe that knowledge can empower the youth into a national asset by providing proper education and guidance.

The Institute runs undergraduate, postgraduate and diploma courses. The institute is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University & Bengaluru North University, recognized by the Government of Karnataka, and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education,New Delhi.

The college is located in Bengaluru, the fastest growing metropolis in India, also know as the Knowledge and BioTechnology capital of India.
